Planets and Person

When we take birth at same moment a snapshot of sky is captured and we got the exact postion of every planets and on this position every planets affect our life. Planets are known as Grahas. There are mainly 7 planets name as Sun, Moon, Mars, Jupiter, Mercury, Venus, Saturn.

The Planets affect the condition of each particular chapter of our lives. There are two more shadow planets name as Rahu and Ketu. all these 9 planets together form navgraha. Each planets have different personalities. we should take care of every ascept of life for having good result of every planet.

Sun represents soul in life. It is the most vital planets in life. A Strong sun amplifies the strength and position in an individual life. Sun represents father.

Moon represents feelings, emotions, subconscious mind and the spiritual nature. Moon indicates mother.

Saturn is the most beautiful planets it is also known as judge of your karma in life. it is farthest and slowest planet.

Jupiter is the planet which is also known as guru of gods. it indicates expansion, healing, good fortune. it is the biggest planet. Jupiter indicates teacher in life.

Venus is planet ruled by Shukra known as guru of deity. venus is the most important planet in present because it represents your marriage life, your lavish lifestyles and kinds of comfort in life.

Mars is important planet in life because it provides the strength of doing any activity in life. It indicates energy, strength, sports.

Mercury is also known as prince of planets. It represents the intelligence, communication ability of an individual.
